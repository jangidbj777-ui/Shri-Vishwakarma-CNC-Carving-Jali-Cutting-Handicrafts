Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your queries

Do you deliver products outside Rajasthan?
Yes, we provide premium packaging and delivery services for custom furniture and carved materials across India.
Can I give my custom design for CNC or Jali cutting?
Absolutely. You can share your own 2D/3D CAD design files, sketches, or photos, and our CNC machinery experts will carve it flawlessly.
What type of wood do you use?
We primary deal in high-grade authentic Teak wood (Sagwan), Sheesham, Premium MDF, Commercial Plywood, and custom timber materials according to budget needs.
